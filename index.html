How we flag pricing. The badges on this site are AgencyNexus's own classifications — our opinion, applied by a fixed formula, not statements of fact about any provider's conduct or intent. TCO is normalized to a 48-month window: intro price × promo term, plus renewal price × remaining months. We label a plan predatory-intro when its renewal runs ≥100% and ≥$5/mo above the intro rate; steep at 50–99%; and ✓ flat when there is no teaser increase. These are structural descriptions of the pricing math; reasonable people may weigh the tradeoffs differently.

Data & accuracy. Prices are a manually compiled snapshot of publicly listed rates (July 2026) and may be incomplete, out of date, or inaccurate; promotional rates change frequently. Always confirm current pricing on the provider's own site before purchasing — do not rely on this tool as the basis for a buying decision. Traffic-capacity figures and the 0–100 performance index are AgencyNexus's own editorial estimates; uptime percentages are each provider's advertised SLA or guarantee, not measured availability. The catalog is data-driven: to add or correct a host, edit its entry in the providers array.
